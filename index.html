Recovery Compass

Evidence-Based Journey Tracking for Sustainable Recovery

Empowering Recovery Through Data

Recovery Compass is a comprehensive platform designed to support individuals on their recovery journey. We provide evidence-based tools for tracking progress, accessing resources, and connecting with support systems.

Our Mission

To democratize access to recovery resources and empower individuals with data-driven insights for their personal recovery journey. We believe that everyone deserves access to the tools and support needed for sustainable recovery.

Key Features

  • Journey Tracking: Monitor your progress with customizable metrics
  • Resource Library: Access evidence-based recovery resources
  • Grant Assistance: Find funding opportunities for treatment
  • Community Support: Connect with peers on similar journeys
  • Privacy First: Your data is encrypted and secure
